About Us

Welcome to Half Fast Farms and Apiary! We are a family of transplants, originally grown in different states, who have found a place to call home in Muskogee, Oklahoma. My wife, a Texas native, brings her rich background in customer service and environmental science. I am Robert, hailing from the picturesque landscapes of Colorado, with extensive experience in agriculture, animal husbandry, and management.

In 2020, during challenging times, we opened our hearts and our little farm to the community to provide quality, farm-to-table food items. For nearly a decade, we have planted our roots in Muskogee, growing not only crops but also community ties.

Our three beautiful children actively participate in daily farm and garden duties, learning the value of hard work and the joy of nurturing life from the ground up. Together, we strive to bring the freshest fruits, vegetables, honey, beeswax products, poultry, and rabbits to your home and table.

Thank you for joining us on this journey. We look forward to growing together!
